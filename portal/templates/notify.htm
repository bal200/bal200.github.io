Notify

Notify everybody in the building that you're outside from a simple tap on your smartphone.

Easy To Use

Every driver in your fleet runs the app in their vehicle. A simple tap of the button as they start their round starts LunchAlert. As the driver arrives at each delivery location a tap of the apps button will send out a notification to all the customers who have LunchAlert installed on their phones.

Promotion

To improve your customer reach promoting LunchAlert to your customers as they purchase their lunch from your vans is important. We've made it really simple for your customers to install and subscribe to your delivery notifications.

Install

Install the notification app on your drivers iPhone or Android smartphones available from the app store.

 

You'll need your LunchAlert registration email and password to log in on the app. These details are provided as part of your subscription set-up.
